Our Story

  • 2012

    The beginnings

    Mariah and Nick met at Virginia Tech, when they joined the same co-ed engineering fraternity, Theta Tau.

  • 2013

    It's official!

    Nick "officially" asked Mariah out after cheering for her at an intramural inner-tube water polo game. He was too nervous at first, so he went back to her dorm 15 minutes later to ask.

  • 2015

    Move to Pittsburgh

    Grad school took Nick to Pittsburgh, PA. The two weren't so fond of being apart, and conveniently Mariah hated her job in VA, so she joined him 6 months later.

  • 2016

    A furry family begins

    Mariah wanted a cat, and Nick didn't... so they compromised and got a cat. Nick now loves Riverâ€”apparently not all cats are jerks.

  • 2019

    Popped the question!

    Nick proposed on the shores of Ruby Beach, along the Olympic Peninsula of Washington State. Mariah had no idea it was coming! Neither remembers what was said.

  • 2020

    Another fur-child

    Not content with a single animal complicating their lives, Mariah and Nick adopted a border collie mix puppy named Felix. He's incredibly energetic, and occasionally adorable.
